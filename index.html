Each urn is hand-thrown on the wheel by ceramicist Nora Hayashi in her Portland studio. The sage glaze is fired at high temperature, producing a matte, earthy finish with subtle variation — no two are identical. Designed to hold the cremated remains of one adult, with a threaded lid for secure, dignified closure.

Earthen Studio believes the objects we use to honor our loved ones should be as beautiful and considered as the people themselves. Every piece is made with that intention.

FAQ

Common questions

Is there a fee to list on CodaCo?+
Starter is free with up to 3 listings. Standard is $12/month with unlimited listings. Pro is $29/month with featured placement. A 5% transaction fee applies on goods sales.
How long does approval take?+
We review all listings within 1–2 business days. Service providers may be asked to provide credentials or verification documents.
Who can list on CodaCo?+
Any US-based individual or business offering goods or services related to death and dying — makers, artists, estate professionals, certified doulas, celebrants, organizers, attorneys, and more.
Can I list both goods and services?+
Yes — you can manage both from a single vendor account. Your profile will display both your goods shop and your service listing.
